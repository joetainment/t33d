Apps for 2D Art
===============


Obviously we don't list all of them below, but these are the most common ones that anyone interested in 2D content authoring should be aware of. Some apps that have 2D features but are primarily 3D editors (such as Blender) are found on the 3D apps page.

Widely used apps that are currently active and are still supported are listed first. Historical apps that aren't widely used anymore or are unsupported/discontinued are listed after.


### FOSS
=========

### Commercial / Proprietary

Adobe Photoshop
        Raster Graphics, basically the standard raster image editor in professional graphics for decades

Adobe Illustrator
         Vector Graphics, basically the standard vector image editor in professional graphics for decades



Procreate
        Digital painting on a tablet with touchscreen pen
        an iPad exclusive app as of 2026-02-28

Adobe Fresco
        raster + vector hybrid for drawing/painting), and 
        
Canva
        freemium, more template-driven but widely used commercially


Clip Studio Paint
        Popular for illustration and comics/manga with a powerful brush engine

Procreate
         iPad-exclusive, one-time purchase. Beloved by illustrators for its natural feel and performance

Affinity Photo
        One-time purchase alternative to Photoshop, well-regarded for professional work without the subscription.

Corel Painter
        Focused on natural media simulation (oils, watercolor, etc.), popular with fine artists

Pixelmator Pro
        Mac-exclusive, one-time purchase, polished and increasingly capable with ML features.
